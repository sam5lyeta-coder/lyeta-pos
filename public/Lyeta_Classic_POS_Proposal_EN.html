Project Proposal

Smart Retail Sales Management & POS System for Lyeta Classic

Project Metadata

Client Name: Lyeta Classic (Retail & Wholesale Business)
Project Name: Lyeta Classic Sales & POS System
Target Industry: General Retail & Wholesale Commerce
Development Stack: Laravel 11 (Backend), React.js (Frontend), MySQL (Database)
Proposed Date: June 2026

1. Executive Summary

Lyeta Classic is a growing retail and wholesale business specializing in high-demand consumer goods. As transaction volumes scale, the existing method of manual bookkeeping and offline paper logs poses challenges to accounting efficiency, inventory accuracy, and financial transparency.

This proposal outlines the implementation of a custom Sales Management and Point of Sale (POS) system. Designed as a lightweight Single Page Application (SPA), the system features real-time inventory adjustments, automatic stock warning updates, cashier audit trails, thermal receipt configuration, and a custom barcode label generator.

2. Problem Statement

Operating without a centralized POS terminal introduces severe business vulnerabilities:

  • Stock Discrepancy: Lack of real-time inventory subtraction leads to stock theft, manual check errors, and product shortages.
  • Profit Margins Obscurity: Net profits are calculated manually at the end of the month, making it difficult to assess day-to-day profit trends.
  • Thermal Receipt Alignment: Standard browsers inject default headers and footers (such as URLs and page numbers) when printing thermal rolls, making receipts look unprofessional.
  • Customer CRM Limitations: Customer records, spent histories, and purchase audits are non-existent, preventing effective loyalty promotions.

3. Detailed Panel & Module Analysis

Here is a comprehensive breakdown of the tabs and features implemented inside the two control panels:

A. Admin Control Panel (Tab by Tab)

1. Dashboard

The central command center for the business owner. It contains:

  • Top Metrics: Three cards displaying "Today's Sales", "Weekly Growth" (percentage change compared to last week), and "Monthly Sales" totals.
  • Weekly Performance Chart: A visual bar chart representing sales trends over the past 7 days, updating automatically based on calendar dates.
  • Low Stock Alert Grid: Highlights products whose stock level is critically low. If all stock is above the threshold, it displays a clean "All stock healthy" status.
  • Live Sales Feed: A real-time log tracking recent purchases, displaying cashier names, payment methods, and timestamps.

2. Cashier Management

A dedicated system panel for managing staff logins. It contains:

  • Cashier Register Form: Form to register new cashier profiles (Name, Email, Password).
  • Permit/Block Actions: Admins can block cashier access instantly or restore permissions.
  • Change Password Form: Allows admins to override and reset staff credentials.

3. Sales Reports

A historical database of all sales. It offers:

  • Audited Logs Table: Lists all checkout logs, payment methods, total amounts, and net profit calculations.
  • Search Filter: Allows searching logs by customer names or transaction dates.

4. Product Stock Control

The catalog inventory manager. It contains:

  • Product Register Form: Form to register products with details such as product name, barcode, stock quantity, buying price, and selling price.
  • Profit Margins Analysis: Automatically displays calculated profit margins (Selling Price - Buying Price) next to each product.

5. Customer Directory

The customer CRM ledger. Features include:

  • 10-Digit Validation: Restricts customer phone inputs to exactly 10 digits, showing validation warnings on incorrect formats.
  • Purchase History Modal: Allows clicking on any customer to view their detailed transaction history, receipt logs, and items bought.
  • Rolling 30-Day Purge: Auto-deletes profiles older than 30 days to protect customer privacy and maintain database speed.

6. Business Progress Report

A weekly audit compiler. It allows downloading a comprehensive PDF report showing product flow (Stock In, Stock Out, Real Stock) and weekly profit margins over a rolling 7-day cycle.

7. Settings & Logins

Allows resetting the admin password, configuring stock warning limits, and downloading barcode label sheets.

B. Cashier Control Panel (Tab by Tab)

1. Dashboard

A restricted view showing today's sales metrics, weekly performance charts, and stock warning notifications.

2. POS Terminal

The primary sales checkout screen. Features include:

  • Product Search & Scan: Cashiers can scan product barcodes or type product names to add items to the cart instantly.
  • Cart Controls: Adjust quantities, view subtotal, Net Amount (Excl. VAT), VAT (18% inclusive/exclusive), and total spent.
  • Payment & Checkout: Select payment methods (Cash, Card, Mobile money), input customer phone number, and complete transaction.
  • Receipt Modal: Toggles printer type profiles (58mm and 80mm rolls) with full-width scaling overrides to prevent margins and browser header/footer leaks.

3. Customer Directory

Allows cashiers to register new customers and view their purchase history.

4. Business Progress Report

A read-only progress status tracking the current week's cycle day.

5. Barcode Generates

Allows cashiers to select a product, input label quantity (starts blank), and generate scannable PDF/browser-printable barcode label sheets.

4. System Integration & Deployment

The backend leverages Laravel's REST API and MySQL's relational integrity. Front-end pages are fully responsive across smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop PCs. All local service queries are optimized to guarantee sub-millisecond response rates under XAMPP environments.
